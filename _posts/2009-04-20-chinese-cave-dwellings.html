Chinese Historic Sites status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- Millions of peasants lived in cave homes cut into the sides of hills. Most lie abandoned today, but I found one still occupied in a village near Luoyang, in Henan Province. It belonged to a family which built it some 200 years ago. The matriarch of the family lived into her 90s and was one of the last women with bound feet. She died about two years ago. Photos by Gary L. Todd, Ph.D., Professor of History, Sias International University, Xinzheng, China. http://picasaweb.google.com/GaryLeeTodd/ChineseCaveDwellings#
