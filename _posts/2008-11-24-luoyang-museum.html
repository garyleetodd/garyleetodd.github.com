Chinese Museums & Artifacts status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- Luoyang was the capital of the Eastern Zhou, Eastern Han, and secondary capital of the Tang Dynasties. It is a rich source of ancient artifacts from myriads of tombs found in the area. The city museum displays artifacts from a long period of Chinese history. Photos by Gary L.
