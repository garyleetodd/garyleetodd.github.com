Iris Chang's, The Rape of Nanking, is an excellent popular account of this forgotten holocaust of WWII. The museum contains many photos which have been published elsewhere, so I did not copy them here. But I have strong sympathies for the Chinese victims of this brutality, as members of my own mother's family perished in the Nazi holocaust at Auschwitz.
