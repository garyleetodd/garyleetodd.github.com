Chinese Historic Sites - Jiangsu - Nanjing status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- Nanjing has a long and fascinating history, some of which is showcased in this and several other Nanjing photo albums on my website. When Zhu Yuanzhang (Emperor Hongwu, 1368-1398) established the Ming Dynasty, he made Nanjing his capital, built the city walls shown here, and built a palace which was later destroyed. The view of Xuanwu Lake Park was taken from the pagoda at Jiming Temple. Photos by Gary L. Todd, Ph.D., Professor of History, Sias International University, Xinzheng, Henan, China. http://picasaweb.google.com/GaryLeeTodd/NanjingMingCityWallsPalaceRuins#
