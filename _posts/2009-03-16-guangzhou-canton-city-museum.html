Chinese Museums & Artifacts status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- Guangzhou City Museum, housed in the Zhenhai Tower in Yuexiu Park, traces the history of the Guangzhou (Canton) area from neolithic settlement through the Qing Dynasty.
