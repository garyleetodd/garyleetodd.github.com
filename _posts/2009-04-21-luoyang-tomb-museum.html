Chinese Museums & Artifacts status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- This is a tomb of a Northern Wei emperor excavated at Luoyang. Nearby some 40 tombs from Henan Province have been reconstructed in a tomb museum. They span over a thousand years from Western Han through N. Song dynasties. The artifacts displayed inside the tombs, of course, are reproductions. Photos by Gary L. Todd, Ph.D., Professor of History, Sias International University, Xinzheng, China. http://picasaweb.google.com/GaryLeeTodd/LuoyangTombMuseum#
