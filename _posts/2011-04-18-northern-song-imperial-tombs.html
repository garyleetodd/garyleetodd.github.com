Chinese Historic Sites status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '3' --- Seven of the nine emperors of the Northern Song Dynasty (960-1127 AD) are buried in and around Gongyi, along with hundreds of royal family members and honored civil officials. Two Imperial tombs are currently protected and open to the public; the others lie scattered among farmers' fields. I have photographed six of the Imperial tombs, along with several lesser tombs. The statues are part of the "spirit way" leading to each tomb. Photos by Gary L.
